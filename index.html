About Me

Before I discovered coding, I had no idea what I wanted to do with my life. I had spent some time in the music industry, tried my hand at acting, even written a novel during quarantine, all with the hope of finding that one thing that makes me excited to get out of bed in the morning. I enjoyed doing all of these things but, to be perfectly honest, they weren't exactly paying the bills.

I ultimately succumbed to my need for food and shelter and decided to try my hand at something a bit more lucrative. I'd heard good things about the tech industry, so I decided to test the waters with a ruby tutorial form codecademy. It left me stunned, shellshocked, starving for more--I was stimulated and exhilerated in ways I'd never been before (no, that rhyme was not intentional, but I do feel it encapsulates my passion pretty well).

There I was, someone who could barely sit still for the space of a breath, spending hours at my desk, clacking away on a keyboard, loving every second of it. This was that thing I'd been looking for, and I couldn't be happier to have finally found it.

In my free time, you'll find me creating or playing video games, watching anime with my wife, or out walking with my two dogs.
